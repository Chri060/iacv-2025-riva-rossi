Each folder includes the following resources:
- angular_speed_graphs: Graphs of the angular speed computed from the two videos.
- 3D_axis: Rotation axis along the trajectory (with Dash app script).
- camera_position: Extrinsic calibration results for both cameras (with Dash app script).
- initial_videos: Original videos used to test the pipeline.
- tracking_videos: Videos showing the tracking results.
- 3D_trajectory:Three-dimensional trajectory of the ball (with Dash app script).
Some folders may also contain subfolders separating the content by camera.
Additional materials are available in the resources folder within the project.
